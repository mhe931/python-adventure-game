Title: Head toward the village
You decide to take the safer route toward the village. As you walk along the well-worn path, you can smell wood smoke and hear the distant sounds of people going about their evening activities.

The village is small but welcoming. Warm light spills from windows, and you can hear laughter coming from what appears to be a tavern. An elderly woman tending her garden waves at you as you approach.

"Welcome, traveler!" she calls out. "Are you looking for a place to rest, or perhaps you're here about the old legends?"
