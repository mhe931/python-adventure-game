Title: Follow the rumbling sound deeper into the cave
Against your better judgment, you decide to follow the mysterious rumbling sound. The crystals grow brighter as you venture deeper, and the cave opens into a massive underground chamber.

There, curled around an enormous pile of gold and precious gems, is a magnificent dragon. Its scales shimmer in the crystal light, and one great eye opens to look at you.

"So," the dragon speaks in a voice like distant thunder, "another treasure hunter has found my lair. But you seem different from the others. You show no fear, only wonder."

The dragon regards you thoughtfully. "I will make you an offer, brave adventurer..."
